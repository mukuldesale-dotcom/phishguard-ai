# 🛡️ PhishGuard AI

AI-Powered Cybersecurity Tool to Detect Phishing Emails, Scam Messages, and Suspicious Links.

Built for Hackathon Submission 🚀

---

## 🔴 Problem Statement

Phishing attacks and scam messages are increasing rapidly.
Students and normal users cannot easily identify malicious emails and suspicious links.

---

## 🟢 Our Solution

PhishGuard AI uses Artificial Intelligence to:

- 🔍 Analyze email content
- 🧠 Detect phishing patterns
- 🔗 Scan suspicious URLs
- 📊 Provide risk score analysis
- 🛡️ Protect users from cyber threats
